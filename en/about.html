About SUPUZZ

A Chinese-owned brand dedicated to creating safe, ergonomic furniture and open-ended STEM toys for children ages 2–8. We serve retailers and distributors worldwide with wholesale, OEM, and custom packaging solutions.

Our Story

SUPUZZ was born from a simple idea: children deserve well-crafted toys and furniture that grow with them. What began as a personal journey to find durable, safe, and imaginative toys for our own kids quickly turned into something more — an independent brand dedicated to quality, safety, and open-ended play.

Today, we design our waffle-style interlocking building blocks and ergonomic kids furniture with the same care we apply at home — thoughtful materials, progressive learning, and products that stand the test of time (and toddler energy).

Our Mission

To empower children to learn, imagine, and problem-solve through open-ended play — and to give parents, retailers, and distributors a trusted partner in building blocks and kids furniture.

We believe that great toys don't over-hype; they quietly support hours of concentration, creativity, and confidence. Whether you're a family shopping for your home or a business looking to bring quality play products to your market, SUPUZZ is here to help.
