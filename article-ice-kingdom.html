Winter Toys

Build Your Own Frozen Kingdom

By SUPUZZ Team

Does your child dream of ice castles, snow queens, and magical winters? The SUPUZZ Ice Series is designed to bring those dreams to life, brick by brick.

Level 1: The Crystal Start (Beginner)

For builders aged 3-4, starting with a massive castle can be overwhelming. Ice Series L1 focuses on simple structures: a small sled, a snow tree, and basic ice crystals. It builds confidence and fine motor skills.

Level 2: The Frozen Village (Intermediate)

Once they master the basics, move to Level 2. Here, children build parts of the village—reindeer stables, larger ice formations, and detailed walls.

Level 3: The Grand Castle (Advanced)

This is the masterpiece. Level 3 combines hundreds of pieces to create a towering Ice Castle with translucent blue bricks that catch the light. It's a project the whole family can enjoy.
