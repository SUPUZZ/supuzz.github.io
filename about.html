SUPUZZ Play With Kids, Not For Kids

Founded in 2023 by Atman, an engineer.

It all started with a simple wish: to share the good toys and furniture he found during his parenting journey—those that are safe, fun, and actually stand the test of messy playtime.

What began as a personal share grew into something more. Today, SUPUZZ is an independent brand crafting kids' products, from ergonomic toddler chairs to open-ended STEM building blocks.

Innovation comes from chance

The best way to accompany kids is to be their playmate and play together

We don’t overdesign, we don’t overhype. We just make products that we’d use with our own kids—products that let kids be kids, and parents join the fun.
