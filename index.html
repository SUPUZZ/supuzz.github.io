Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is SUPUZZ suitable for? +

SUPUZZ Flower Garden Building Toys are designed for children ages 3-8 years old. The starter kit works great for toddlers (3-5), while the deluxe sets offer more complexity for older kids. Always supervise young children during play.

Are SUPUZZ toys safe for toddlers? +

Yes! All SUPUZZ products are made from 100% non-toxic, BPA-free ABS plastic. Pieces are large enough to prevent choking hazards and have smooth, rounded edges. All products meet or exceed safety standards and are regularly tested.

How do I clean SUPUZZ toys? +

Super easy! Just wipe down with a damp cloth and mild soap. All pieces can be washed and are completely waterproof. Let them air dry. Avoid dishwashers and extreme heat sources.

Can pieces from different sets be mixed together? +

Yes! All SUPUZZ flowers use the same connection system. You can mix and match pieces from the Starter Kit, Worlds, and Ultimate sets to create even more combinations.

What if pieces go missing or break? +

Replacement piece packs are available for purchase on Amazon. Our sets are built to last, and pieces rarely break with normal use. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products.

How quickly will my order arrive? +

All SUPUZZ products sold on Amazon are Prime eligible. Standard delivery is 1-2 days with Prime membership. Non-Prime orders typically arrive within 5-7 business days. Check your Amazon order for exact delivery estimates.
